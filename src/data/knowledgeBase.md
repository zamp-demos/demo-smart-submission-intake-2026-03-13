# Chubb Financial Institutions D&O — Smart Submission Intake
## Knowledge Base

### Process Overview
This workflow automates the intake and triage of D&O Financial Institutions renewal submissions. Pace processes the submission from inbound email through regulatory verification, financial analysis, and premium indication — delivering a quote-ready package to the underwriter in under 10 minutes.

### Key Systems
- **Chubb PAS** — Policy Administration System. Used for incumbent clearance, loss history retrieval, data population (61 fields), and triage tagging.
- **FDIC Enforcement Actions Portal** (orders.fdic.gov) — Primary source for consent order verification.
- **SEC EDGAR** — Document verification and 8-K material event scan.
- **FFIEC UBPR** (cdr.ffiec.gov) — Bank-specific peer benchmarking data.
- **Chubb Rating Tool** — FI-specific premium calculation model.

### Common Questions

**Why no BSA/AML exclusion?**
The consent order is terminated. Applying an exclusion would penalize a resolved issue. Pace recommends a regulatory rep/warranty endorsement instead.

**Why maintain $1M retention?**
The one prior claim ($850K) was within retention and Chubb paid $0. The claim is closed with prejudice. Maintaining retention is appropriate.

**Is the CRE concentration a concern?**
At 278%, it's elevated relative to the 300% regulatory guidance threshold but is below peer median (295%) and declining from 291% in FY2023. It warrants a note but does not trigger referral.

**What governance improvements did Pinnacle make?**
